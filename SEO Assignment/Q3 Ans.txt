Q3. What are the Benefits of Using Meta Tags

Ans :- 
      Meta tags are snippets of code that provide information about your web page to search engines and users.

      Benefits of Using Meta Tags:-

      - They can help you optimize your site for SEO and improve your click-through rate.
 
      - Search engines can use your keywords as signals of your page's topic and content, 
        helping them index and rank your page more accurately and relevantly.

      - Viewport control - The viewport meta tag is crucial for responsive web design.

      - SEO - (Search Engine Optimization): Meta tags, such as the "description" and "keywords" 
        tags, can help improve a webpage's visibility on search engine result pages (SERPs).
